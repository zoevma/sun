Pumpkin Bread


This makes a lot of pumpkin bread, I'd halve the recipe or so...

Ingredients
  • 15 ounces pumpkin puree
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 2/3 cup water
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3 1/2 cups flour
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp nutmeg
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp ground cloves
  • 1/4 tsp ginger
Directions
  1. Preheat oven to 175 degrees C and grease the pan(s).
  2. In a large bowl, mix the ingredients from pumpkin puree to sugar together.
  3. In a seperate bowl, whisk together the rest. Stir the dry ingredients into the pumpkin mixture. Pour into the prepared pan(s).
  4. Bake for about 50 min, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Enjoy!
