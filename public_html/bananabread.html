Banana Bread


This recipe is great if you have a few old bananas.

Ingredients

1 cup mashed bananas
1 cup sour cream
1/4 cup butter
1/2-1 cup sugar
2 eggs
3 1 tsp vanilla extract
2 cups all purpose flour
1 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp salt
1 tsp baking soda

Directions

1. Preheat oven to 175 degrees C. Grease and flour a pan.
2. Combine banana and sour cream. Set aside.
3. In a large bowl, beat the butter and sugar until smooth. Beat in eggs one at a time, add vanilla extract and the banana mixture. Spread the batter evenly into the prepared pan.
4. Bake for 40-50 min, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Enjoy!
