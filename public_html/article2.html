Blueberry Buckle


Ingredients

3/4 cup sugar
1/4 cup butter
1 egg
1/2 cup milk
2 cups all-purpose flour
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 cups fresh blueberries
1/2 cup sugar
1/3 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/4 cup butter, softened

Directions

1. Preheat oven to 190 degrees C. Grease a 8x8 inch pan.
2. Cream together 3/4 cup sugar, butter and egg.
3. In a seperate bowl mix together 2 cups flour, baking powder and salt. Stir into sugar mixture, alternating with milk. Stir in blueberries and pour into the pan.
4. To make topping, combine 1/2 cup sugar, 1/3 cup flour, cinnamon and butter. Sprinkle over cake batter.
5. Bake for 25-30 minutes. Enjoy!
