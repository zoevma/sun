Chocolate Cookies


Ingredients

1/2 cup butter, softened
1/2 cup soft, light brown sugar
3/4 cup sugar
1 egg
1/2 cup chocolate spread
2-3 drops vanilla extract
1 2/3 cups flour
1/3 cup cocoa powder
1 pinch salt
1/2 tsp baking powder

Directions

1. Preheat oven to 175 degrees C, cream the butter and sugar together in a large bowl.
2. Using the mixer, beat the egg, chocolate spread and vanilla extract in to the sugar mixture.
3. Sift the flour, baking powder and salt in to a bowl.
4. Add the sifted flour mixture to the chocolate mixture and gently mix them together. Stir in chocolate chips, sprinkles or whatever you want.
5. Distribute the batter on baking trays and bake for 9 minutes. Enjoy!
